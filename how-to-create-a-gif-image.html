Animated GIFs have been around for a while, but they are gaining popularity.

Making an animated GIF is easy and only requires a few simple steps.


First, you need to find an image or video that you want to use.

You can search online or use one that you already have saved on your computer.

Once you have the image or video, open it in a photo or video editing program.


Next, you will need to select the portion of the image or video that you want to animate.

Then, add any text or other effects that you want.

Finally, save your file as a GIF.

That's it!

Now you can share your animated GIF with friends and family.
