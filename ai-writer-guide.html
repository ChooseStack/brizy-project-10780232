Introduction: Defining AI writers

When it comes to artificial intelligence (AI) writers, there are a few different types to choose from. Here is a quick rundown of some of the more popular AI writers on the market today:


  • GPT-2: GPT-2 is an open-source AI model that was released by OpenAI in February 2019. It is a successor to the original GPT model that was released in 2017. GPT-2 is a large transformer-based language model that can generate human-like text.


  • GPT-3: GPT-3 is one of the most advanced AI writers available. It can generate realistic text in a variety of styles, making it ideal for things like creating fake news articles or generating realistic dialogue. However, GPT-3 is also very expensive to use.



  • OpenAI Text Generator: The OpenAI Text Generator is another advanced AI writer, but it’s open source and therefore free to use. It can generate realistic text in a variety of styles, making it ideal for things like creating fake news articles or generating realistic dialogue.

What to consider when choosing an AI writer ?


When it comes to choosing an AI writer, there are a few things you should take into consideration.

First and foremost, what is your budget?

There are many AI writers on the market, and their prices can vary greatly. If you have a limited budget, you may want to consider a less expensive option.

Secondly, what do you need the AI writer for?

Are you looking for something that can generate content for you quickly and easily?

Or are you looking for something that can help improve your writing skills?

There are AI writers that specialize in different areas, so be sure to choose one that will meet your needs.

Finally, take some time to read reviews of AI writers before making your decision.
