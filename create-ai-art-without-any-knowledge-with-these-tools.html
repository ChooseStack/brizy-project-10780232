Create AI Art without any knowledge with these nocode tools

Introduction: brief explanation of what AI art is

AI art is a new and exciting field of art that is created using artificial intelligence.

AI art can be created using a variety of tools and techniques, and it can be used to create both traditional and abstract art.


Many people believe that AI art will eventually replace traditional art, but this is not necessarily the case. AI art can be used to complement traditional art, or it can be used to create entirely new and unique pieces of artwork.


If youre interested in creating AI art, there are a few different tools you can use.

One popular tool is TensorFlow, which is an open-source library for machine learning (but not easy to use because you have to build your model first !).

Another tool you can use is GANBreeder, which allows you to breed different images together to create new images.

But we will see more no-code tools in a moment.

No matter what tool you use, anyone can create AI art without any prior knowledge or experience.

Process: step-by-step guide on how to create AI art

To create AI art, you don't need to be a tech expert or an artist. All you need is a willingness to experiment and explore. With the right tools, anyone can create stunning AI-generated art.


Here's a step-by-step guide to creating your own AI art:


1. Choose a tool. There are many AI art creation tools available online, such as Deep Dream Generator, MidJourney, ArtBreeder, StableDiffusion. Pick one that looks fun and easy to use.


2. Follow the tool's instructions. Each tool is different, but most will require you to upload an image to serve as the basis or a Prompt for your artwork. Or follow our guide about AI generators


3. Let the tool do its work. Depending on the complexity of the image you've chosen, it may take a few minutes or hours for the AI to generate your artwork.


4. Enjoy your creation! Most tools will allow you to download the AI artwork in a variety of formats, including JPG. You can also share it on social media or embed it into your website. AI art is an exciting new genre of digital art.
